CPSC 583 Project Variation 3

Film Genre, Revenue, Rating, and Runtime. Data Source

The third variation attempts to address the issue of it being difficult to really see any significant correlations between the revenue and genre or even between the films. Therefore, the x axis is changed into a logarithmic scale. This helps to see the points that are too close together, but still keep the extremes. The main visualization is an extension of this and addresses the design to make it more aesthetically pleasing.
